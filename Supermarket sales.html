Visualization and Analysis of Supermarket Sales

For a Supermarket mall, We have to keep track of Total Sales, Profit, and Products Quantity. In this interactive dashboard, I created all types of details such as Monthly sales, Daily sales, Top products, Sales types, and Top categories to keep an eye on Supermarket sales and Take impactful and strong decisions to grow the business.

The Insights of The Supermarket Sales Dashboard are highlighted below,

  • Total Sales, Profit, and Profit Percentage.
  • Monthly and Daily sales.
  • Type of Sales and Payment mode.
  • Top selling products.
  • Top Category.
  • Category-wise sales distribution.
  • Year and Months Filters.
