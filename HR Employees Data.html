Visualization and Analysis of HR Data

For an HR manager, the attrition rate is a vital KPI to keep an eye on. In this project, a tableau-based dashboard is designed to render insight into attrition rates in different departments of a company.This dashboard is designed with the intention of providing maximum insight into HR attrition data in a blink of an eye. The Main focused KPIs are,

  • Total Employees of company.
  • Active Employees.
  • Attrition Count.
  • Attrition Rate.
  • Attrition by Gender.
  • Department wise Attrition.
  • Employee Job Satisfaction Ratings of different job roles.
  • Attrition by Education Group.
